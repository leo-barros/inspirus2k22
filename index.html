The Council of Computer Engineering Students (C-CODES) in association with the Department of Computer Engineering is pleased to announce the 6th edition of its all-Goa event, INSPIRUS, on the 3rd and 4th November 2022 .
INSPIRUS for the last 5 years has been the flagship technical event of Don Bosco College of Engineering and it has found massive success in participation from various schools, higher secondaries, and colleges across Goa who come together to compete, innovate and create, year after year, to strive to be the best and to win massive prizes!
