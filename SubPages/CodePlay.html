Code Play
Eligibility Criteria
  • Students from High School, Higher Secondary, Undergraduate and Diploma from professional non-professional institutions.
Rules
  • The students participating should be from the same institution (Interdepartmental teams allowed).
  • A PC with Microsoft Visual Studio installed for C/C++ will be provided to each team.
  • This event will have the teams of 2 participants each divided as coders and players. So each team will consist of a coder and a player.
  • Coders:They will be required to generate the expected outputs for the given problem statements.
  • Players:They will compete in mini games (simple games like, minute to win it and other fun games).
  • Round 1: - The coders have to frame the logic for some simple problem statements, code it and submit. Simultaneously the second player will compete in an obstacle course of mini games. The time taken by both the coders and players will be added together. The top 12 with the fastest time and efficient working codes will be considered for the next round.
  • Time Duration:
  • For the obstacle course:10 minutes
    For coding: 45 minutes
  • Round 2: - The problem statement will be divided into many parts. The players will have to retrieve each part of the problem statement one at a time through various mini games and hand it over to their partners until the complete problem statement is formed. The coders will simultaneously be coding while receiving each of the parts of the problem statement. Points will be assigned to each of the teams based on their final codes. The Top 6 with the highest points will progress to the next round.
  • Time Duration: 45 mins
  • Round 3: - This will be the toughest round where the top 6 teams will compete to win. In this round both the coders and players will compete together in 2 person games and will have to collectively work on the problem statement given, both the team members will have to code. This round will have many suprise elements and the complete procedure of this round will be disclosed only on the day of the event.
  • Time Duration: 45 mins
  • In case a tie arises between teams, a tie breaker round will be conducted to select the winner.
  • Note: 15 minute breaks will be provided after each round.
  • Total Time Duration: 3 hrs

  • Rounds 2 and 3 depend on the no. of participants, any changes will be conveyed to the applicants.
Judging Criteria
Logic, Accuracy, Indentations, Readability, Implementation, Speed.
Skills required:Speed, hand eye coordination, agility, creativity, promptness, basic knowledge in c and c++.
