Incarnate!
Eligibility Criteria
  • Undergraduate students (FE, SE, TE, BE) in all streams from all participating institutions, Higher secondary, High school students
Rules
  • The drawing needs to be an animated adaptation of an already existing live-action character from a Netflix show/documentary/movie
  • Along with the drawing, a brief description of the character is also expected
  • Participants will have 20 hours to draw the character, and can only use drawing tablets, keyboards and mouses
  • Use of all illustrative software is permitted. However, the use of software with readily available resources is not allowed and will lead to the disqualification of the participant
  • Plagiarism checks will be made. Any participant found to be plagiarizing will be disqualified
  • The drawing should not contain any lewd content. All such entries will be disqualified
  • Further details will be given before the event
  • In case of any disputes or discrepancies, the decision made by the Organizing Committee will be final
Judging Criteria
Creativity, Originality, Uniqueness, Quality of artistic composition and overall design based on the theme.
