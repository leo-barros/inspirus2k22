Drop the Beat
Eligibility Criteria
  • Undergraduate and Diploma students from all professional and non-professional institutions.
Rules
  • All participants must register online.
  • Participants are allowed to use any mixing software for the virtual round but have to strictly use either Virtual DJ or Virtual Studio software for the 2nd round(Live Set).
  • Maximum Number of Participants: 15


  • There will be 2 major rounds :
  • 1) Online Submissions
  • This round will be conducted online.
  • A curated playlist will be given to you, by 10 am, 3rd November 2022, which is to be mixed using any software of your choice. The final mix should be between 4 to 5 minutes and include some element of every song provided.
  • The final mix should be submitted by 4 pm, 3rd November 2022. The finalists will be announced by 7 pm.
  • The top 4 mixes will be chosen for the Live Set round.
  • 2) Live Set
  • This round will be held offline at Don Bosco College of Engineering, Fatorda, on 4th November 2022. All finalists will have to be physically present on campus.
  • You will have a maximum time of 15 minutes to play a set (no premixing allowed) using Virtual DJ or Virtual Studio software.
  • Participants can choose their own songs for their live set.
  • DJs will be judged based on audience reactions as well as judges.
  • Further details will be conveyed via a Whatsapp group once the team registers.


  • Note:
  • Participants will be disqualified if they are unable to attend the 2nd round when selected for the same.
  • The registration form should be filled out only once.
  • If a participant commits any form of malpractice they will be disqualified immediately.
  • Any foul/vulgar/offensive language will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • We will contact you through the contact you have provided in the registration form.
  • In case of any query feel free to approach the coordinators on WhatsApp/Call.
Judging Criteria
  • Creativity , Accuracy mix sequence , crowd reaction.
