Drop the Beat
Eligibility Criteria
  • Undergraduate and Diploma students from all professional and non-professional institutions.
Rules
  • All teams must register Online
  • Participants are allowed to use any mixing software for the virtual round but have to use virtual dj or virtual studio software for the live round
  • There will be 2 Major rounds :
  • i. Online Submissions:
  • A playlist will be given which is to be remixed using software of your choice. The final mix should be between 4 to 5 minutes and include a part of every song provided. The top 4 mixes will be chosen for the live event on 4th November 2022.
  • The final mix should be submitted by 1st November 2022
  • ii. Live round
  • This round will be held offline in the Don Bosco Clg. of Engg., Fatorda campus
  • You will have a maximum time of 10 minutes to play a set (no premixing allowed) using virtual dj or virtual studio Software. Participants can choose their own songs for their live set.
  • The 2 DJs with the best audience reactions and judge scores will be awarded.
  • Further details will be conveyed once the team registers.
  • If a participant commits any form of malpractice they will be disqualified immediately.
  • Any foul/vulgar/offensive language will lead to immediate disqualification.

  • Note :-
  • Participants will be disqualified if unable to attend the live event when selected for the same.
  • The registration form should be filled by the team leader only once.
  • We will contact you through the contact you have provided in the registration form
  • Incase of any query feel free to approach the coordinators on WhatsApp / Call.
Judging Criteria
  • Creativity , Accuracy mix sequence , crowd reaction.
