Reel It, Feel It!
Eligibility Criteria
  • Students from High School, Higher Secondary, Undergraduate and Diploma from professional non-professional institutions.
Rules
  • Inspiration can be drawn from the existing trends online, but plagiarized content will lead to disqualification.
  • Any derogatory remarks, name-calling, and/or regional slang towards any person or community should not be used.
  • The use of offensive and inappropriate language or actions will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The video should not exceed 1 minute.
  • Participants should submit only one reel, multiple entries will lead to disqualification.
  • The video should be made vertically and not horizontally. The preferred aspect ratio is 9:16.
  • Participants will be given a 3-second video template which has to be included before the reel starts, in which they have to mention their name and entry number.
Theme
(Preliminary round)
Participants are free to choose any one of the following:
  • Day in my life
  • Goa through my lens
  • Cinematic edits
  • Movie spoofs
  • Ad-making (for a product/service)


(Final round)
The theme will be announced after shortlisting the top 9 participants.

Procedure
(Preliminary round)
1. After successful registration, participants will have to post their reel on their Instagram account (After adding the entry details template).
2. Participants have to post the reel on their Instagram account and add @inspirus.reels as a collaborator in the post, mention the entry number, and participant name in the caption along with ‚Äò#inspirus2k22‚Äô and tag @inspirusdbce
3. Entries will be judged based on the criteria mentioned and the number of likes and comments on their reel. (1 point is given for every 50 likes & unique comments ie no duplicates, capped at 10 points)
4. Number of likes and comments as of 30th October (11:59 pm) will be considered.
5. Top 9 entries will proceed to the final round.

(Final round)
1. Final round will be held offline therefore participants will have to be present on 3rd November 2022 in the college.
2. The theme for the final round will be announced after shortlisting the top 9.
3. Participants will be given 24 hrs to make the reel, which they have to submit via a Google Form before 10 am on 4th November 2022.
4. Top 2 participants will be awarded cash prizes and winner certificates.
Judging Criteria
  • Creativity, Originality, Retention, Editing and Number of likes and comments on your reel (Engagement)
