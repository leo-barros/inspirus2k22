Mind Games
Eligibility Criteria
  • Students from High School, Higher Secondary, Undergraduate and Diploma from professional non-professional institutions.
  • 1 team per college, HSS
Rules
  • Registration will be through an online form.
  • The form will be closed on 1st November 2022 OR if the maximum number of participants is reached.
  • The registration form should be filled out by the team leader only once.
  • Any team submitting more than one form will be disqualified.
  • Only the team leader should join the WhatsApp group via the link provided after the submission of the form.
  • All teams should report to the venue by 1:45 pm.
  • Round-specific instructions will be provided before each round.
  • The event will be made up of 3 rounds.
  • The first and second rounds will be made up of riddles, ciphers, and logical or aptitude questions.
  • Based on the cumulative score of the first two rounds, teams will be eliminated and only the top 5 teams will go to the final round.
  • The final round will require each team to solve each clue and find the key to the treasure.
  • All teams will receive different clues and will be located at different places, with the final clue leading to a key to the treasure.
  • All teams should take the clues along with them.
  • The first 2 teams to find their keys will be awarded 1st and 2nd place accordingly.
  • While searching the clues around the college, team members must keep in mind to not disturb the ongoing classes and also must not enter the restricted areas, anyone found doing so will be immediately disqualified.
  • Hiding/tampering with other teams‚Äô clues will lead to disqualification.
  • In case of discrepancy, decisions made by the coordinators will be final.
  • All team members should carry their ID cards.
